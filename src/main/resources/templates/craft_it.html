CRAFT IT
DESCRIPTION

Craft It is a fun and challenging event where participants have to build a working mechanism using only cardboard and basic tools. The event unleashes the creativity, engineering skills, and problem-solving abilities of the participants.

RULES
  • Each team must have Minimum of two and Maximum of three members.
  • Blueprint for the mechanism will be provided by the instructors.
  • Cardboard and components will be provided by the instructors.
  • The competition consists of two-level difficulty in mechanism and the more weightage will be provided for the most Difficult mechanism.
  • Participant must complete both mechanism within 1 hour.
  • Participants are responsible for maintaining cleanliness in their workplace and in minimising the wastes.
  • Safety measures should be followed at all times.
  • Each team must have some knowledge about Theory of machines.
