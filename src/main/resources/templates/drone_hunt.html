DRONE HUNT
DESCRIPTION

Embark on an exhilarating adventure with the Drone Hunt Challenge! Compete against skilled pilots in a thrilling race to track and capture elusive drones. Navigate through obstacles, showcase your precision, and demonstrate strategic prowess to emerge victorious. Join us for an adrenaline-fuelled experience like no other!

RULES
  • Size of the drone must be 600 mm x 600 mm.
  • Maximum permissible weight for the drones 250 grams (If you have the valid licence).
  • Launch pad will be provided for the drone.
  • The time the time count will be commences at the starting of the drone from the launch pad.
  • The drone must cross the 10-hindrance provided in between the launchpad and the end point and return back to the launchpad.
  • If the drone fails to complete the 10 hindrances, it must start from the beginning of the launch pad but the time will be counted in account.
  • Only one person is allowed to operate the drone from the starting to the end of event.
  • Mobilisation of the drone in between the event will leads to disqualification.
  • The participant will be given a trial before commencement of the actual event.
  • Judges' decision shall be treated as final and binding on all.
