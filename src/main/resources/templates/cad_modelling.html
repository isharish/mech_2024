CAD
MODELLING
DESCRIPTION

A mechanical CAD modelling competition challenges participants to create precise, innovative designs using computer-aided design software, showcasing their skills in engineering, problem-solving, and creativity within defined parameters for judging.

RULES
  • You have a time limit of one hour to complete the design work.
  • Competitors have the freedom to use any modelling software you are comfortable with. Mention the software which your comfortable during registration.
  • Systems will be provided from our side; usage of participant's laptops is also allowed.
  • The design should be accurate and precise, with all measurements and components in their rightful places.
  • Collaborations or any discussions among the participants are not allowed during the competition; one must work individually on their design.
