WATER
ROCKETRY
DESCRIPTION

The Water Rocketry Challenge is a thrilling competition where teams of aspiring engineers and rocket enthusiasts showcase their ingenuity, teamwork, and passion for exploration, combining physics, engineering, and creativity in a splashy spectacle.

RULES
  • A team can only consist of one or two participants.
  • Participants are only permitted to use pressure-resistant plastic bottles or pressure-tested ones, such as carbonated-drink bottles.
  • The bottle can be adorned with optional accessories such as (fins and nose cones).
  • If the teams' models are determined to be unsafe in any manner, they will either be rejected (No glass or sharp objects should be used in making the rockets. Use of electronic components and chemical explosives is banned.)
  • While multiple bottles are not permitted, a bottle's maximum capacity should be 2.5 litres.
  • The rocket's only source of energy can be the combination of air pressure and water.
  • There can be no other energy source.
  • Water and hand pumps will be provided. The hand pumps are appropriate for both the bicycles and bike valves.
  • Rocket Launching pads will be provided.
